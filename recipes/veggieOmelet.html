Veggie Omelet

Description

A veggie omelet is perfect for anybody who wants to get enough protein while getting some cooked, delicious vegetables. There are many different recipes for a veggie omelet, however this one will specifically use red and green peppers, mushrooms, onions, and spinach. They also vary in size, but this recipe results in a medium-sized veggie omelet. Please note that you don't need have the exact ingredients, and you can very the amount of vegetables as you wish.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1/2 a cup of sliced red peppers
  • 1/2 a cup of sliced green peppers
  • 1 cup of sliced onions
  • 1/2 of mushrooms
  • A pinch of spinach
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs and scramble them in a bowl
  2. Add salt and papper to the scrabled egg mix
  3. Get a skillet and turn the stovetop on to a medium to high temperature
  4. Once hot, add the 2 tablespoons of olive oil in the skillet
  5. Add the sliced veggies in the skillet and let them cook for around 3 minutes
  6. Add the scrambled egg mix in the skillet and let it simmer there for 2 minutes
  7. When the edges start to form, use a spatula to lift the omelet mixture to make sure the omelet does not stick to the pan.
  8. Once the omelet is done enough, flip it on itself and enjoy!
