How to make chocolate chip cookies!

Description

This recipe is heavily inspired from Betty Crocker with a few notable changes. This recipe is perfect for baking warm, gooey chocolate chip cookies! Following this recipe will give you chocolate chip cookies that are around medium-sized. Additionally, this recipe makes around 20 to 25 cookies. The main ingredients will be the Betty Crocker's Chocolate Chip Cookie Mix along with a lot of chocolate chips!

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of Betty Crocker's Chocolate Chip Mix
  • 1 stick of softened salted butter
  • 3 teaspoons of water
  • 4 teaspoons of rich, milk-chocolate morsels from Nestle

Steps

  1. Preheat your conventional oven to exactly 425 degrees
  2. Gather all of the ingredients and get a mixing bowl a mixing utensil
  3. Dump the Betty Crocker's Cookie Mix into the mixing bowl
  4. Soften the stick of unsalted butter by warming it up in the microwave for around 20 seconds
  5. Put the 3 teaspoons of water in the mixing bowl
  6. Add the milk-chocolate morsels in the mixing bowl
  7. Mix all ingredients until the mixture is wet and solid
  8. Get a large oven pan and cover it with a baking sheet
  9. Using a medium-sized ice cream scooper, scoop out the cookie mixture and put the cookie dough on the sheet. Make sure each cookie dough ball is at least 2 inches apart from each other.
  10. Put the cookies in the oven for around 10 minutes, or until the edges are golden-brown
